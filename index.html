Our Story

When Earth
Meets Light

In a world pressed on fast-forward, our studio is where time slows down. LumiClay was born from a simple wish: to find the light hidden within clay.

"Lumi" means light. "Clay" means earth. The beauty of ceramics lies in its transformation — from silent, humble soil into something luminous through the alchemy of fire. This journey from darkness to glow is the philosophy we live by.
