The Story

Forged in Fire,
Worn in Stillness

Deep beneath the surface, volcanic stone forms over millennia — compressed by immense pressure, tempered by heat that would consume most things. What emerges is something profoundly calm: dense, smooth, and grounding.

We select each piece of lava stone by hand from ethically sourced quarries, then shape and polish them into beads that carry the quiet weight of geological time. Paired with hand-glazed ceramic accent beads, this bracelet is both ornament and talisman.

Living With Your Piece

Care Guide

water_drop

Water-Friendly

Lava stone is naturally water-resistant. Rinse under cool water and pat dry. Avoid soaps and chemicals.

spa

Essential Oils

The porous stone absorbs essential oils beautifully. Add 1–2 drops of lavender or eucalyptus for a calming scent.

brightness_5

Sun Exposure

Brief sunlight is fine, but prolonged UV can fade ceramic glazes. Store in a cool, shaded place when not wearing.

inventory_2

Storage

Keep in the included linen pouch. Lay flat rather than hanging to prevent elastic cord from stretching over time.
